This elegant canine breed is very intelligent, fiercely loyal, and always alert. The Doberman, also known as the German Pinscher, is strong and tenacious with some streaks of stubbornness. They are generally non-aggressive to their owners, mildly aggressive to other canines, and very aggressive to strangers.
